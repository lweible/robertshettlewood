Who am I?

I'm Robert Shettlewood, a Cultural Researcher, Brand Manager, Stylist and NYU graduate, based in New York City. I grew up between Atlanta,GA and Queens, New York. Having access to both cities helped my curiosity and creativity flourish. I weave my taste for cultures, fashion, music and art into creating and executing ideas. My biggest goal for my work is to create authentic connections between brands and underrepresented communities and consumers.
